Market Structure:

Monopolistic Competition

From Humble Beginnings to a Great Success

Our idea for this general partnership sparked from a simple conversation at home. My sister is well-known in our family for the artist in her while I was known for my knowledge in the field of technology. My sister simply told me: "Yo, make me a personal website!", and I replied with "Sure, why not?" And thus, T Solutions was born.

It was the perfect setup. She would be the front-end designer and I would be the developer. We've been able to create well-crafted websites on our own. Together, we have the potential to create aesthetic and functional websites and applications that would be useful for businesses such as our family's. Today, everyone is on the internet, so it is only ideal to create a website to expand your business and widen your reach!

Getting into the industry wasn't that hard as all we needed was a laptop and stable internet connection. However, it is the development skills, functional design and quality of service that sets each web development company apart. This is why our market structure is monopolistic competition. We have the same product as the rest of the web development firms: a website. It is in the quality of the websites and services and in the marketing strategies where we differ from one another.

Our competition only includes the biggest web development firms in the Philippines such as Asiagate Networks, Inc., Optimind Technology Solutions, Syntactics, and hundreds of more firms in the country. They simply develop websites and marketing solutions for the huge firms. On the other hand, we do our best to connect with our clients in order to establish and maintain a long-term relationship from which both parties would benefit. They may have the advantage in numbers, experience and services; however, I believe that my sister and I have this bond that would set our product apart from the rest.

We have set our price range to Php 4,000 to 10,000 because we don't want our clients to adjust to us. We adjust to our clients. We are open to clients ranging from new startups to major corporations in the market. Given the diversity of finances, we aim to be flexible towards our clients and give them just the right package they need. So inquire now, because we've got everything here for you!
